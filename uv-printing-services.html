  • Materials

  • What materials can be used for UV printing in UAE?
    UV printing supports vinyl, metal, acrylic, wood, and selected fabric surfaces depending on job size and finish.

  • Outdoor Use

  • Is UV printing waterproof for outdoor signage?
    UV inks are durable and suitable for outdoor usage when paired with the right media and protective finishing.

  • Comparison

  • How do I choose between UV print and sticker lamination?
    UV is preferred for direct rigid-surface printing, while laminated stickers are used for flexible adhesive applications.
